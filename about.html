Who We Are

Driving Innovation in Chemistry

Kodai Chemicals India Private Limited is a leading name in the chemical distribution and manufacturing landscape, dedicated to bridging the gap between global chemical innovation and local industrial application.

Founded on the principles of integrity and quality, we have evolved from a trading house into a comprehensive solution provider. We serve a diverse clientele ranging from multinational corporations to local enterprises in the Paints, Plastics, and Construction sectors.

Our unique hybrid model allows us to source hard-to-find raw materials from global markets while also manufacturing specialized formulations at our state-of-the-art facility in Maharashtra.
